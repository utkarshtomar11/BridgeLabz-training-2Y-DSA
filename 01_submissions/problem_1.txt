c) Logical Error in Algorithm B
The second loop divides sum by n a total of n times, computing sum / n^n instead of sum / n. The correct average requires dividing the total once by n. This violates the mathematical definition of arithmetic mean: x̄ = Σxᵢ / n (one division, not n divisions).
